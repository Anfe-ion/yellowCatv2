yellowCat e-Bike Warranty Policy

Warranty Coverage:

yellowCat offers a limited warranty for all new e-bikes purchased through our store. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for a period of 12 months from the date of purchase.

What's Covered:

  • Frame: Structural defects in the frame.
  • Battery: Manufacturing defects or issues with battery performance.
  • Motor: Malfunctions or defects in the motor.
  • Electrical Components: Issues with the controller, display, and wiring.
  • Mechanical Components: Defects in parts like brakes, pedals, or gears.

What's Not Covered:

  • Normal wear and tear.
  • Damage resulting from accidents, misuse, or neglect.
  • Modifications or unauthorized repairs.
  • Damage caused by improper assembly or maintenance.
  • Cosmetic damage, including scratches or dents.

Claim Process:

To make a warranty claim, please contact yellowCat customer service with your proof of purchase and a detailed description of the issue. We may request photos or videos to assess the problem.

Warranty Service:

If your claim is approved, yellowCat will, at its discretion, repair or replace the defective part or the entire e-bike. The replacement parts or e-bike may be new or refurbished.

Limitations:

This warranty is non-transferable and only applies to the original purchaser. It does not cover incidental or consequential damages. The warranty is void if the e-bike has been used for commercial purposes.

Shipping Costs:

The customer is responsible for shipping costs associated with warranty claims. If the claim is approved, yellowCat may, at its discretion, cover the return shipping costs.

Contact Information:

For warranty claims or questions, please contact us at support@yellowCat.com.
