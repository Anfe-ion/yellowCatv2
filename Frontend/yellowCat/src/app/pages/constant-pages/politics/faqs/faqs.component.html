yellowCat E-Bike FAQs

1. What is the warranty on yellowCat e-bikes?

All yellowCat e-bikes come with a 12-month limited warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. This includes the frame, battery, motor, and electrical components.

2. How do I make a warranty claim?

To make a warranty claim, contact our customer service at support@yellowCat.com. Please provide your proof of purchase and a detailed description of the issue. Photos or videos may be requested to help assess the problem.

3. What is not covered under the warranty?

The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, damage from accidents, misuse, or neglect, unauthorized modifications, or cosmetic damage like scratches or dents.

4. How long does it take to process a warranty claim?

The processing time for a warranty claim varies, but we strive to resolve issues within 7-10 business days after receiving all necessary information.

5. Do I have to pay for shipping when making a warranty claim?

Yes, the customer is responsible for shipping costs to return the product for warranty service. If the claim is approved, we may cover the cost of return shipping at our discretion.

7. Can I use my yellowCat e-bike for commercial purposes?

Our e-bikes are designed for personal use. The warranty is void if the e-bike is used for commercial purposes, such as rental services or deliveries.

8. How should I maintain my yellowCat e-bike?

Regular maintenance includes checking tire pressure, cleaning the chain, and ensuring the battery is properly charged. Refer to the user manual for detailed maintenance instructions.

9. Where can I get replacement parts for my e-bike?

Replacement parts are available through our online store. If you need assistance, please contact our customer service team.

10. Can I upgrade my yellowCat e-bike?

Yes, you can customize or upgrade your e-bike with compatible parts. However, any modifications that are not performed by an authorized technician may void the warranty.
